# Task Results Review

You are reviewing the results of multiple tasks that
were executed to address a research query.

## Instructions

Review the task results and evaluate:

1. **Completeness**: Are all tasks adequately
   addressed?
2. **Quality**: Is the information accurate and
   well-reasoned?
3. **Coherence**: Do the results connect logically?
4. **Gaps**: Are there any missing perspectives or
   information?

### Handling Non-English Text

- Attempt to translate cited writings and work titles into English when possible.
- When appropriate, provide the original text in the original language alongside the English translation.

## Output

Provide a structured review that:

- Highlights strengths of the task results
- Identifies any weaknesses or gaps
- Notes patterns or connections between tasks
- Recommends any improvements or additional context
  needed

Your review will be used to inform the final summary
generation.
